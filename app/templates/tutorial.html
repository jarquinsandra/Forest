Consensus calculator

This application was designed for the analisis of mass spectrometry data, the application needs normalized to base peak txt files to work.

It was developed by Dra. Sandra Martínez Jarquín for the US-Forest Service and the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service Forensics Laboratory as a part of a bigger application for the analysis of DART-MS spectra obtained from trees.
You may use your files from another organism and ionization technique as long as they fill the requirements mentioned before, in case you find and error, a bug or if you have any suggestions you can reach me at jarquinsandra@gmail.com
